💬 "But My Borrower Won't..."

Tap to see how Debbie handles it. Chad just gives up.

😰 "Is it safe to log into my bank through your app?"
Debbie: "You're not giving us your login. It uses the same tech as Venmo and every banking app. Over half of Americans have used it. It's encrypted, and we never see your password. Way more secure than emailing bank statements."
📄 "I'd rather just send you my bank statements."
Debbie: "Bank statements are 30-60 pages, they always have wrong dates, and if one page is missing we start over. This pulls exactly what we need in real time. No scanning, no missing pages, and it shaves up to 2 weeks off your closing."
🤷 "Can't the processor just handle this?"
Debbie: "They can. But here's why I do it at application: if I order verifications upfront and the file is clean, I can bypass the processing queue entirely and submit straight to underwriting. My processor doesn't have to chase docs, and I get a same-day review. The processor handles it later? That's two extra weeks in the queue."
🤔 "What if there's a large deposit?"
Debbie: "We'd see that on paper statements too, and we'd have to ask about it either way. The digital report actually makes it easier to explain because we can see the deposit source and pattern. If it's a gift, we handle the gift letter up front. No surprises later."
